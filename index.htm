iOS

Couch Game

REQUIREMENTS:

Works on both iPhone and iPad (iOS7 or greater).

INSTRUCTIONS:

Drag couch around by touch to avoid falling objects.

Tap couch to shoot fire bullets at falling objects to gain points.

MECHANICS:

Objects fall at random locations and speeds.

More objects fall, and speeds increase as the game progresses.

Faster objects are worth more points.

Smaller objects are worth more points.

Cat meows, dog barks, and bear Roooaaarrrs

Lose points for falling objects that go past without getting hit.

Lose points for inaccuracy; fire bullets that don't hit anything.

Background changes every 2000 points.

KNOWN ISSUES:

High Score is reset when app is closed (sorry, plan to put in Game Center for v3).

REWARDS:

Players who give me unique and useful feedback, suggestions and/or discover issues will be rewarded monetarily.
