  • skatecreteordie

    skate'crete or die is a skatepark locator map for world class, concrete skateparks. solid black pins represent world class parks, clear filled black pins represent other parks, and yellow filled pins denote skateparks that have lights. tap a pin to see its skatepark name, then tap the details button in the bottom tab bar to see details for that skatepark. tap the photo to download and view the next additional photo (if there are more than one). this app can be run without an internet connection; however, photos will not appear, unless they have been downloaded beforehand and the app has not closed. note: currently there are only washington state skateparks on the map. more will be added for future revisions of skate'crete or die. there will also be more photos and descriptions.
